Frequently Asked Questions

I’m on a free account, why should I switch to a paid account?

Your free account will only give you access to a limited part of the courses. When you take a subscription, you can access all courses and tutorials unlimited.

How should I decide on what course to take? Is prior knowledge required?

Every course has a difficulty level and an estimated time to completion. Based on your interests and knowledge level you can choose what course to take. No prior knowledge? Take our free Introduction To R course.

I need help :-s

Every course and tutorial has a discussion board where you can ask questions and get help. If you have a particular question or feedback, just shoot us an email at mgmt@datacamp.com.

What are the system requirements?

To get the optimal experience make use of a browser such as Chrome, Safari or Firefox. IE10 or higher are supported as well. We recommend you to take your courses on a laptop or desktop PC.

Do you offer team subscriptions?

Yes. Creating a data-driven company culture is hard. With DataCamp you can train the data scientists and data savvy managers you need. Check out our team page.

I am a student/active in academics/work at a non-profit.

As a student, academic personnel or non-profit employee you get a discount when subscribing to DataCamp. Check out the student page.
