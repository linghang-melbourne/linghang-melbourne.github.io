Like the information you enter at registration or in your Profile, cookie and log file data is used to customize your experience on the Web site. Due to the communication standards on the Internet, when you visit the Haltbox Web site, the URL of the site from which you came is automatically noted, as is the site to which you are going when you leave Haltbox The Internet protocol (IP) address of your computer (or the proxy server you use to access the World Wide Web), your computer operating system and type of Web browser you are using, as well as the name of your ISP is also noted.
